Learning Outcomes

By successfully completing this course, students will be able to:
  • Identify numbers and signs.
  • Solve addition, subtraction, division, and multiplication problems.
  • Identify symbols and operations.
  • Solve fraction, decimal and percent problems.
  • Summarize basic algebra terms.
  • Solve linear equations and inequalities.
  • Solve algebra word problems.
  • Demonstrate solving algebra problems with simplification.
  • Solve algebraic fraction problems.
  • Define lines, points, and space in geometric terms.
  • Summarize geometric concepts and relationships.
  • Identify the Pythagorean Theorem.
  • Summarize measurements of congruence and similarity.
  • Summarize coordinate geometry.
  • Solve geometric problems involving perimeters and areas.
  • Solve geometric problems involving volumes and surface areas.
  • Demonstrate mastery of lesson content at levels of 70% or higher.
